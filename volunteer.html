    • VOLUNTEER

    How to volunteer:
    1. Fill out the CFBISD Volunteer Form (required for all volunteers by CFBISD)
    2. Sign up for volunteering through CHARMS
    Why should I volunteer?

    Parent participation through volunteering provides support to the band directors. The Boosters handle many tasks allowing the directors to focus their attention on the students and instruction. Volunteers are able to spend time with the students and admire first hand their progress and development.

    Want some more reasons to help out?

    • You're helping your child as well as their friends succeed
    • You get to meet awesome people
    • Improved self-esteem
    • Networking

    Below are some fun and easy ways to get involved in the Turner Band.

    • Chaperones for games, competitions, trips, lock-ins
    • Pit Crew to help with percussion instruments, props, sound equipment, loading and unloading instruments/equipment are on wheels
    • Concessions for Tuesday Middle School Games and Friday night home games. This is one of the biggest money-makers for the band!
    • Water Distributor at games and contests
    • Bleacher Clean-up crew after games
    • Prop design and construction
    • Printing
    • Meal servers for the band before home games
    • Meal cooking for directors on long days
    • Summer band fruit cutters for band breaks
    • Assisting at feeder school band instrument drives
    • Truck driver for away games and competitions
    • Luggage monitor to keep an eye on all luggage before loading for a trip
    • Merch Booth at events (band spirit wear, pit crew salsa) Fund Raising goods distributor (cookie dough)
